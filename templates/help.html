Instruction manual


  1. Open the browser and run the application.

  2. Click on the “Browse” option to upload the desired video.

  3. It directs you to the file explorer.

  4. Choose any video from your local computer.

  5. Make sure the file format is a valid video format (like .mp4, .avi, .wmv etc).

  6. Do not exceed the upload size limit of 200 MB per file.

  7. Click on the Open button once you choose your desired video.

  8. The preview of the video opens up on the screen.

  9. If the desired video has not been chosen then go back and repeat the procedure from Step 2.

  10. Click on the Process button to anonymise your video.

  11. The anonymised video will be visible.

  12. “Anonymized Video successfully exported to original path” is displayed on your screen once the video is anonymised.

  13. Go to the file location on your computer to view the anonymized video saved with name "video_processed.mp4"
